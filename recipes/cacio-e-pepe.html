Cacio E Pepe

Description:

Cacio e Pepe literally translates to "cheese and pepper". It is of italian in origin and while it is super simple, it is extremely delicious!

Ingredients:

  • Pasta of choice (bucatini or spaghetti recommended)
  • Pecorino Romano
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. Boil pasta to cook to a perfect al dent, about 6-8 minutes
  2. Drain pasta, saving about 1 cup of the pasta water
  3. add drained pasta back to pot and stir in 2 tablespoons of butter
  4. Grate plenty of cheese on to pasta and add some pasta water and Pepper
  5. Stir until a cheesy creamy sauce is formed
  6. Plate the pasta and grate more cheese on top and solt and pepper to taste.
